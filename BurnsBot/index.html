🔥🔥 Auto liquidity token burn works through a process called deflationary tokenomics, which is designed to decrease the total supply of a token over time. This process is typically achieved through a combination of two mechanisms: automatic liquidity provision and token burning.


🔥🔥 Auto liquidity Token burning refers to the process of destroying a certain amount of tokens directly from the pooled supply.This is achieved by sending the tokens to a "burn address" that is owned by the smart contract and cannot be accessed by anyone. By reducing the total supply of the token, the value of each remaining token should theoretically increase, assuming demand for the token remains constant.
